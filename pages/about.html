White Cove - About.

White Cove is a lovely two bedroomed dog friendly, cottage that sleeps four in one double room (with an ensuite toilet, sink and shower room) and one twin room on the ground floor. All linen and towels are provided. All the duvets and pillows are hypoallergenic but feather pillows are available on request. Both bedrooms have space for putting clothes and bags away. There is a travel cot also available on request.

The property has hard wood flooring throughout which is easy to keep clean with all the sand that comes in and benefits from central heating. The stairs lead to the open plan first floor space with a kitchen/diner and living room with great sea views where you can watch the sun rise and storms blow in. There is a separate bathroom on this level with a bath, toilet and sink.

The kitchen is well stocked with modern appliances including a washing machine, dishwasher, full size fridge freezer, electric oven, gas hob, microwave, and coffee machine. There is a kitchen table that seats 4-6 people with accompanying chairs and a high chair available on request.

The living room also benefits from comfortable sofas (two and three seaters) digital TV with freeview, a DVD player and a Bluetooth and wireless compatible digital radio. We also supply some board games, DVDs and books for you to use during your stay with wireless internet access throughout.

From the living room there is a set of steep, paddle steps which lead to the mezzanine floor/snug where you can continue to enjoy the views. *Please take care on these steps they are very steep and quite awkward to descend. Please keep an eye on young children to avoid any accidents.

There is a vacuum cleaner and mop for your convenience, and cleaning supplies will be left under the kitchen sink for your arrival along with washing up liquid, cloths, tea towels, hand soap and soap for the dishwasher and washing machine should you need them.
