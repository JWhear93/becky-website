Bookings.

Please email us for availability (including late deals and short breaks) and booking information or use the contact form below.

Prices range from £424 per week in low season to £1,245 per week in high season and no booking fees. However, we will need to take a £150 security deposit which will be refunded to you after your stay subject to a housekeeping check. Short breaks are available outside of high season.

To secure a booking, a 25% booking deposit will be needed. This can be paid by online transfer. A booking will be confirmed on receipt of the electronic transfer of the deposit with the rest to be paid 6 weeks before the booking start date.

Dogs will be charged at an extra £20 fee per stay.
